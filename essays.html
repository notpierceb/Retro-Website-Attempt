Capitalism and nature
x

https://valleysunderground.files.wordpress.com/2020/04/blackshirts-and-reds-by-michael-parenti.pdf (p.154)

Ive noticed how a byproduct of capitalism is a monetary-based social hierarchy.

I've always deemed this type of hierarchy the most threatening phenomenae for our planet because for someone in a capitalistic society to fulfill their innate desire to climb the social hierarchy, they must develop an entirely exploitative relationship with nature.

In his essay, Michael Parentis deems the capitalism's "raison d’être" is to "convert nature into commodities and commodities into capital." Essentially transforming the animate earth into inanimate wealth.

"When the last tree has been cut down, the last fish caught, the last river poisoned, only then will we realize that one cannot eat money."
