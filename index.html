Nice to meet you!

Welcome to my website!!!! Whether you're from Europe, America, or wherever, you can always come to my small chunk of the internet.

"So, why did you make this site?" you might ask, well, this is simply my small tribute to the vibrant internet of the past. :)

I love the internet, but I dislike how over time, its focus shifted from a place of vibrant personal expression to a bland corporate hellscape. Reversing this phenomenon is the motivator for creating this site. Basically so I could be cool like the olden days.

Anyways, more about me, I'm Pierce! On a good day, you might find me eating junk food, watering my plants, or both at the same time! I am a second year computer science student, and I also love playing my guitar!

On my endlessly under-construction website, you might find anything related to me and my hobbies, such as music, space stuff, academic side projects, literature and art I like, and my guitar covers!

Books corner?

Not a very big reader but I do enjoy a fun novel every now and then! Here are my recommendations..

The Golden Compass by Phillip Pullman - Easy page turner read, and the ending was amazing.

The Girl on the train by Paula Hawkins - Intense murder mystery told from the perspective of an unreliable narrator.

If It Bleeds by Stephen King - 3 unconventional and thought-provoking horror novellas

Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy by Douglas Adams - Reading it is always a good time.
